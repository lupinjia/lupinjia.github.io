## Abstract

Train a quadruped robot parkour policy using RL. The end-to-end policy receives depth image and proprioceptive observation as input and outputs desired joint angles.

## Demo Video

The Unitree Go2 with trained policy can climb up 0.5m platform and jump across 0.6m wide gap, shown in the below video.
